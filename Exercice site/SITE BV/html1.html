The Nightmare Before Christmas (ou l'étrange Noël de M. Jack)

(eng) Here we are it's spooky season, yet again. Haloween also means sweets, costumes, but also... MOVIES! And that is why I present to you one of my favourite movies of all timr: The Nightmare before Christmas. A little context: released in 1993, the film was made using stop motion, and it only made 3 years to make! If this seems long to you, just know that capturing only a few seconds in a smooth way can take up to hours or even days. This is one of my favourite movies EVER, from the story or the characters to the musics (scroll down for an exemple).
